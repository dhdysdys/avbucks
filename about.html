History

There was store in Jakarta that offered some of the world's finest fresh-roasted bean coffees. This store already famous and have many branch.

Our chairman, Lanse Jack, a chinese-indonesian walked and buy some coffee in this store. Lanse was drawn into this store and joined a year later became part-timer.

In 2018, Lanse left that store and travelled to Italy and became capitaved by Italian coffee. He also learned how to make espresso. He also travelled to small farms in Africa, Latin America, and the Pacific in search of the best coffee the world has to offer.

In 2019, Lanse return and start his own cafe that right now called AVbucks.

Today, with more than 10 markets, AVbucks is the premier roaster and retailer of specialty coffee in Indonesia. And with every cup, we strive to bring both our heritage and an exceptional experience to life.
